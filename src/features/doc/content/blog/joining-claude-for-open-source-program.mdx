Some happy news to share: I’ve been accepted into the [Claude for Open Source Program](https://claude.com/contact-sales/claude-for-oss) by Anthropic.

The email opened with a simple “You’re in” and honestly, that made my day. As part of the program, I’ll receive **Claude Max 20x** free for 6 months. It’s Anthropic’s way of thanking open source contributors who, in their words, “keep the ecosystem running”.

<FramedImage
  src="https://assets.chanhdai.com/images/blog/joining-claude-for-open-source-program.webp"
  alt="Welcome to the Claude for Open Source Program"
/>

I applied with my open source projects, including two that the community has been kind enough to embrace:

- [chanhdai.com](https://github.com/ncdai/chanhdai.com): a developer portfolio template and shadcn registry with over 2,000 stars on GitHub. It has inspired many developers in the community to build their own portfolios.
- [React Wheel Picker](https://github.com/ncdai/react-wheel-picker): a clean, mobile-style picker for React applications. It’s lightweight, smooth, and easy to drop into any form, with 30,000+ weekly downloads and 700+ stars on GitHub. The project is also sponsored by Vercel through the [Vercel OSS Program 2025](https://vercel.com/blog/summer-2025-oss-program#react-wheel-picker).

Claude has already become a big part of how I build, from shipping new components to reviewing code in my daily workflow. Having Claude Max for the next 6 months means I can spend more time doing what I love: building, polishing, and giving back to open source.

A huge thank you to Anthropic for supporting open source developers like me. It’s a real encouragement to keep maintaining and improving these projects.
